# AMLA 2020 Compliance Mapping

The Anti-Money Laundering Act of 2020 (AMLA 2020) encourages financial institutions to adopt innovative technological approaches to combat illicit finance. EvoAML directly addresses these regulatory priorities.

## 1. Addressing the "Innovation" Mandate (AMLA 2020 Sec. 6207)
**Challenge:** Traditional rule-based systems generate high false-positive rates and fail to detect evolving typologies.
**EvoAML Solution:** The Temporal Evolution Analysis engine adapts to new money laundering patterns automatically, fulfilling the FinCEN mandate for dynamic risk-based methodologies.

## 2. Cross-Jurisdictional and Multi-Sector Visibility (AMLA 2020 Sec. 6101)
**Challenge:** Funds moved across supply chain networks often escape siloed institutional monitoring.
**EvoAML Solution:** The Graph-Driven Tracking module connects fragmented transactional data across industries (e.g., energy, mobility), providing a holistic view for law enforcement and regulators.

## 3. Actionable SAR Generation (BSA Requirement)
**Challenge:** Highly technical AI models are often "black boxes" that investigators cannot interpret.
**EvoAML Solution:** The `compliance_adapter` transforms GNN weights and attention scores into human-readable Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) narratives, ensuring transparency and auditability.
